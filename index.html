How to Play TypeRacer
Game Rules:
  1. Select your preferred difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard).
  2. Start typing in the text box to begin the test automatically.
  3. Type the exact text shown above, including punctuation and capitalization.
  4. Words will be highlighted in:
    • Blue - Correctly typed words
    • Red - Incorrectly typed words
    • Black - Pending words
  5. The test will automatically stop when you correctly complete the text.
  6. Your results will show:
    • Current difficulty level
    • Time taken in seconds
    • Words Per Minute (WPM)
Tips:
  • Practice regularly to improve your typing speed
  • Focus on accuracy first, speed will come naturally
  • Use the Retry button to practice with different texts
